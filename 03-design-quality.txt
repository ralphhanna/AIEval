Evaluate the design quality of this code.

Focus on:
- separation of concerns
- coupling between responsibilities
- cohesion within functions, classes, and modules
- whether boundaries are clean or blurred
- whether the structure supports future change
- whether logic is placed at the right level of abstraction
- whether the code mixes validation, orchestration, persistence, formatting, networking, or UI concerns in the same place

Scoring guidance (0-10):
- 9-10: strong structure, clear boundaries, good separation of concerns
- 7-8: reasonable structure with some design weaknesses
- 5-6: noticeable coupling or mixed responsibilities
- 3-4: poor structure, blurred boundaries, hard to evolve safely
- 0-2: no discernible design, everything entangled
