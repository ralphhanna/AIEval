Evaluate the dependency health of this repository.

Focus on:
- number of dependencies relative to project scope
- presence of known deprecated or abandoned dependencies
- lock file presence and consistency (package-lock.json, yarn.lock, etc.)
- dependency scope correctness (dev vs production)
- version pinning strategy (exact, range, or unpinned)
- duplicate or redundant dependencies
- supply chain risk indicators (very new packages, single-maintainer critical deps)
- whether dependencies are actively maintained
- whether unused dependencies are present

Important:
- Base your assessment on visible manifests (package.json, requirements.txt,
  go.mod, Cargo.toml, etc.) and lock files.
- Do not run audit tools; assess from the manifest contents.
- If no dependency manifest is visible, score conservatively and explain.

Scoring guidance (0-10):
- 9-10: lean, well-maintained, properly scoped and pinned dependencies
- 7-8: reasonable dependencies with minor issues (a few unpinned or stale)
- 5-6: some concern — redundant, outdated, or poorly scoped dependencies
- 3-4: notable dependency issues, missing lock files, abandoned packages
- 0-2: severe issues, no manifest, or critically vulnerable dependencies
